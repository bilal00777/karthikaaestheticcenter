Karthika Nair

Founder, Karthika’s Professional Beauty Clinic
Kochi, India

When Karthika Nair moved to Kochi from a small village in Palakkad nearly a decade ago, the thought of starting her own business was far from her mind. As the daughter of a middle-class family, with her father successfully running a modest hotel business, her goals centered around achieving financial independence. Karthika’s journey began when she took a part-time job with a public relations company. Her exceptional marketing skills quickly caught the attention of her boss, who encouraged her to pursue a future in the field.

Starting with a humble salary of Rs. 4,000 per month, Karthika embraced the world of marketing with enthusiasm. After several years of hard work, she received a pivotal opportunity from a leading Italian cosmetic brand that was launching in India. Although Karthika accepted the offer, she insisted on understanding the science behind the cosmetics before fully committing. This drive led her to receive international training, allowing her to become an expert in skincare and beauty consultancy.

As she marketed these Italian products, Karthika became increasingly aware of the lack of professionalism and expertise in India’s beauty industry. She was particularly disturbed by the widespread and careless use of bleach, which could cause long-term skin damage. This realization inspired her to create a beauty clinic that would prioritize educated and safe skincare practices. Thus, in 2016, at the age of 27, Karthika launched Karthika’s Professional Beauty Clinic in Kathrikadavu, Kochi. Despite earning a comfortable salary of Rs. 78,000 at the time, Karthika took the bold step of investing all her savings and securing an Rs. 18 lakh loan to make her dream a reality.

Her determination paid off after six months of intensive planning and preparation. The clinic, which opened on June 15, 2016, became the first in the state to reject the use of harmful bleach treatments. Her philosophy, “healthy skin and hair for a lifetime,” resonates deeply with her clients.

Karthika’s Professional Beauty Clinic has since expanded, with a new franchise opening in Aluva, Ernakulam on February 12, 2018. Karthika is not only passionate about her clients’ well-being but also about her staff’s development. Every new employee undergoes a 21-day training program to ensure they meet the clinic’s high standards of care and professionalism.

In addition to her success in the beauty industry, Karthika has ventured into the restaurant business with her new venture, Le Royale. Her entrepreneurial journey continues to evolve, but her dedication to promoting “healthy skin and hair for a lifetime” remains at the core of her work.
