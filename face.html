Face Treatments

HydraFacial

A non-invasive, customizable facial treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates your skin for a healthy glow. It helps improve the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, congested and enlarged pores, and oily or acne-prone skin.

Micro-needling

Micro-needling is a minimally invasive procedure that uses tiny needles to create controlled skin injury. This treatment helps in reducing fine lines, acne scars, and improving overall skin texture.

Chemical Peeling

Chemical peeling is a skin-resurfacing procedure where a chemical solution is applied to remove the top layers of the skin. It helps in treating wrinkles, skin discoloration, and scars, leaving the skin smoother and less wrinkled.

Dermabrasion Facial

Dermabrasion is a type of exfoliating treatment that uses a rotating instrument to remove the outer layers of skin, often used to treat facial lines, sun damage, and acne scars.

Medi-Facials

Medi-facials are specially designed medical-grade facials that use powerful ingredients to deeply cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate the skin, resulting in a rejuvenated and radiant appearance.
