Acne and Scar Treatment Overview

Acne and scar treatment at Karthika Aesthetic Centre aims to reduce acne severity, prevent future breakouts, and minimize scarring. Our personalized approach combines advanced techniques to restore a healthy complexion and improve skin texture.

Acne Treatment Process

Our acne treatment process involves a range of methods to address both active acne and prevent future occurrences:

  1. Assessment: A dermatologist evaluates the type and severity of acne to determine an appropriate treatment plan.
  2. Topical Treatments:
    • Benzoyl Peroxide: Reduces bacteria and prevents clogged pores.
    • Salicylic Acid: Exfoliates and unclogs pores.
    • Retinoids: Increases cell turnover.
    • Antibiotics: Reduce inflammation and bacteria.
  3. Oral Medications:
    • Antibiotics: For moderate to severe cases.
    • Hormonal Treatments: Regulate hormones in women.
    • Isotretinoin: For severe acne unresponsive to other treatments.
  4. Procedures:
    • Chemical Peels: Exfoliate the skin to reduce acne.
    • Laser Therapy: Targets active acne and reduces inflammation.
    • Extraction: Manual removal of blackheads and whiteheads by a dermatologist.

Scar Treatment Process

Our scar treatment process helps reduce the visibility of acne scars and improve skin texture:

  1. Assessment: A dermatologist evaluates the type and severity of scars to determine the best approach.
  2. Topical Treatments:
    • Silicone Gel Sheets: Flatten and smooth scars.
    • Retinoids: Improve scar appearance by promoting cell turnover.
  3. Procedures:
    • Microdermabrasion: Exfoliates the outer layer of skin.
    • Chemical Peels: Enhance skin texture and tone.
    • Laser Therapy: Resurfaces scar tissue to promote new skin growth.
    • Microneedling: Creates micro-injuries to stimulate collagen growth.
    • Fillers: Raises depressed scars for a smoother appearance.
  4. Surgical Options:
    • Scar Revision Surgery: Reshapes or removes scars surgically.

Benefits of Acne and Scar Treatment

Reduces Breakouts and Scarring

Our treatments help minimize active acne, reduce inflammation, and improve the overall appearance of scars.

Prevents Future Acne Formation

By targeting acne triggers and promoting skin health, our treatments help prevent future breakouts.

Improves Skin Texture

We use exfoliation and collagen-boosting techniques to enhance skin texture and promote regeneration.

Boosts Self-Esteem

Clearer skin can lead to improved self-confidence and a positive outlook on life.
