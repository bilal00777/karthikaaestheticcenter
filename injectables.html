Injectables Treatments

Botox

Botox is an injectable treatment that temporarily reduces the appearance of facial wrinkles and fine lines by relaxing the muscles underneath. It is commonly used on the forehead, around the eyes, and between the eyebrows for a smoother, youthful look.

Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are used to restore lost volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial contours. These injections are effective in rejuvenating skin and can help in treating smile lines, plumping lips, and enhancing cheeks for a youthful appearance.

Lip Fillers

Lip fillers are a type of dermal filler specifically designed to add volume to your lips, providing a fuller, plumper look. This treatment is perfect for enhancing lip symmetry and defining the natural lip line.

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

PRP therapy uses the patient's own plasma to rejuvenate the skin and stimulate collagen production. It is effective for treating wrinkles, acne scars, and improving skin texture, giving a refreshed, youthful glow.

Mesotherapy

Mesotherapy involves injecting a mixture of vitamins, enzymes, and other nutrients into the skin to rejuvenate, hydrate, and tighten it. This treatment is used for skin rejuvenation, fat reduction, and even hair loss treatment.
