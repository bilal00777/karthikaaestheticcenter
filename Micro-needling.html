Micro-Needling Treatment

At Karthika's Aesthetic Centre, we offer micro-needling treatments that rejuvenate your skin and scalp by promoting collagen production and stimulating hair growth. Our micro-needling procedures are customized for both the face and scalp, designed to enhance your natural beauty and health.


Why Choose Our Micro-Needling?

  • Skin Rejuvenation: Micro-needling stimulates the production of collagen and elastin, reducing the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and scars, resulting in smoother, more radiant skin.
  • Improved Texture and Tone: Micro-needling helps improve skin texture and tone by minimizing large pores and hyperpigmentation, creating a more even and refreshed complexion.
  • Hair Growth Stimulation: Micro-needling can be used on the scalp to stimulate hair growth, enhance blood circulation, and improve nutrient delivery to hair follicles, promoting healthier hair growth and reducing hair loss.
  • Enhanced Product Absorption: Micro-needling creates micro-channels in the skin, allowing for better absorption of topical skincare products and hair serums, maximizing their effectiveness.

Benefits of Micro-Needling

  • Skin Rejuvenation: Stimulates collagen and elastin production, reducing the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and scars.
  • Improved Skin Texture: Reduces the appearance of large pores and hyperpigmentation for smoother, more even skin.
  • Hair Growth: Stimulates hair follicles on the scalp to promote healthier hair growth and reduce hair loss.
  • Better Product Absorption: Allows for deeper penetration of skincare and hair care products, improving their results.

Who Can Opt for Micro-Needling?

Micro-needling is suitable for individuals looking to improve their skin's texture and tone or promote hair growth. Whether you are seeking to reduce fine lines, acne scars, or stimulate hair regrowth, micro-needling is a versatile option for both skincare and hair restoration.
