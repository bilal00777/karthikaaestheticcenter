Welcome to Karthika's Aesthetic Centre

Karthika's Aesthetic Centre is a premier clinic offering a wide range of modern skin and hair treatments. Our experienced team provides personalized care and utilizes state-of-the-art technology to help you achieve your aesthetic goals. From facial rejuvenation and laser treatments to hair restoration and body treatments, we offer comprehensive services in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Founded in June 2016 by Karthika Nair, our centre has grown into one of the most trusted destinations for personalized and high-quality aesthetic treatments. Karthika Nair, a highly regarded industry expert, brings over 7 years of experience as a business consultant. In 2019, she was honored with the prestigious "Young Leaders Creating a Better World for All" award by the Women Economic Forum.

Before starting the clinic, Karthika served as a Technical Consultant for a renowned international Italian cosmetic company, where she was recognized as the best employee. Her expertise and dedication to excellence have been the foundation of Karthika's Aesthetic Centre, ensuring that every client receives the best possible care and results.

At Karthika's Aesthetic Centre, we believe in a patient-centric approach. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities, and we strive to help you achieve your desired look through tailored treatments and ongoing support.
