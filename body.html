Body Treatments

Deep Nourishment

Deep nourishment treatment for the body involves using rich, nourishing ingredients to hydrate and rejuvenate the skin, leaving it soft and smooth. It helps in replenishing moisture and providing essential nutrients to your skin.

Anti-Hair Fall Treatments

Our anti-hair fall treatments target the underlying causes of hair loss, using customized products and therapies to strengthen hair roots and reduce breakage, leaving you with thicker, healthier hair.

Medi Spa Treatments

Medi Spa treatments offer a relaxing and therapeutic approach to body care. Combining spa luxury with medical expertise, these treatments help in addressing skin concerns, detoxification, and overall body rejuvenation.

Dandruff Treatment

Our dandruff treatment involves specialized care to reduce scalp dryness and flakiness, using products that soothe and hydrate the scalp to prevent dandruff reoccurrence and restore scalp health.

Laser Hair Restoration

Laser hair restoration is a non-invasive treatment that uses low-level laser therapy to stimulate hair growth, improve blood flow to the scalp, and strengthen hair follicles, promoting healthier hair growth.

Laser Hair Reduction

Laser hair reduction is a long-term solution for unwanted hair. Using advanced laser technology, this treatment targets hair follicles to inhibit future hair growth, leaving your skin smooth and hair-free.
