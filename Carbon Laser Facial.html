Carbon Laser Facial Treatment

The Carbon Laser Facial at Karthika's Aesthetic Centre is a revolutionary, non-invasive treatment that helps achieve radiant and clear skin. Often referred to as the "Hollywood Peel," this treatment utilizes laser technology combined with a carbon-based lotion to exfoliate, cleanse, and rejuvenate the skin. It is ideal for people looking to reduce oiliness, clear clogged pores, and rejuvenate the skin.


Why Choose Our Carbon Laser Facials?

  • Deep Cleansing: The carbon particles penetrate deep into the skin, binding to dirt, oil, and impurities for a deep cleanse that refreshes and revitalizes your skin.
  • Non-Invasive: This treatment is safe, painless, and requires no downtime, making it perfect for individuals looking for a quick yet effective solution.
  • Immediate Results: Experience smoother, more radiant skin after just one session with long-term benefits when part of a regular skincare routine.
  • Reduces Oiliness: Effective for oily skin, as it helps reduce sebum production and tightens pores, thereby controlling acne.

Benefits of Carbon Laser Facials

  • Improves Skin Texture: Helps to exfoliate dead skin cells, leaving skin smoother and more refined.
  • Minimizes Pore Size: Helps to reduce the appearance of enlarged pores and control excess oil production.
  • Reduces Acne and Scarring: Can help diminish active acne and lighten acne scars for a more even complexion.
  • Brightens Complexion: Provides an instant glow, making the skin appear more vibrant and youthful.

Who Can Opt for Carbon Laser Facials?

The Carbon Laser Facial is suitable for individuals with oily skin, large pores, or acne-prone skin. It is also beneficial for anyone seeking to reduce fine lines, brighten their complexion, or even out their skin tone. Due to its gentle, non-invasive nature, it is suitable for most skin types, but we recommend consulting with our skincare professionals to determine if it is the right treatment for your needs.
